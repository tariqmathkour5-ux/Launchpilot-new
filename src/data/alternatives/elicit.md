## Top 10 Elicit Alternatives

### 1. Perplexity AI

**Pricing**: freemium

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine that provides direct answers with citations. It combines search with AI to give comprehensive, sourced responses to queries.

**Key Features**:
- Web search integration
- Cited sources
- Follow-up questions
- Multiple AI models

**Best For**: Academic research

**Why Choose This**: Perplexity AI offers a free tier and Real-time information with API access.

### 2. Perplexity Pages

**Pricing**: free

Perplexity Pages allows users to create structured, comprehensive content based on AI research with proper citations.

**Key Features**:
- AI-powered research
- Citation management
- Structured content
- Publishing

**Best For**: Research articles

**Why Choose This**: Perplexity Pages offers a free tier and Cited research.

### 3. Consensus

**Pricing**: freemium

Consensus is an AI-powered search engine specifically for scientific and academic research, providing direct answers from peer-reviewed papers.

**Key Features**:
- Scientific paper search
- AI-powered answers
- Study snapshots
- Paper filters

**Best For**: Academic research

**Why Choose This**: Consensus offers a free tier and Academic sources only.

---


### 4. Semantic Scholar

**Pricing**: free

Semantic Scholar is a free AI-powered research tool for scientific literature, developed by the Allen Institute for AI.

**Key Features**:
- AI paper search
- Citation analysis
- Related papers
- Research feeds

**Best For**: Academic research

**Why Choose This**: Semantic Scholar offers a free tier and Completely free with API access.

---


### 5. Connected Papers

**Pricing**: free

Connected Papers is a visual tool that helps researchers find and explore papers relevant to their field of work using graph visualization.

**Key Features**:
- Visual graph exploration
- Related paper discovery
- Citation analysis
- Prior work identification

**Best For**: Literature review

**Why Choose This**: Connected Papers offers a free tier and Visual exploration.

---


### 6. You.com

**Pricing**: freemium

You.com is a privacy-focused AI search engine that provides direct answers with citations and multiple AI modes for different tasks.

**Key Features**:
- AI search answers
- Multiple AI modes
- Code assistant
- Image generation

**Best For**: AI-powered search

**Why Choose This**: You.com offers a free tier and Privacy-focused with API access.
